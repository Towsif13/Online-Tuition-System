About Us

Online tuition system is basically a common platform for both interested tutor and students. Here,the students and teachers have been categorized differently.Both the parties need an email id and password for signing up in the website. They will be providing information in their own profile. Besides general information, there will be information such as – classes, subjects, present tutors for students and tutoring status, academic background, institution for the teachers.According to their provided information the students are able to find their desired teacher and vice- versa.Both the users can change or update their profile anytime. The students are able to see the teacher’s profile and so the teachers. There is a messaging option where students and teachers are connected through messages. The admin of the website will have control over the whole process. He will be able to see the profiles of both parties and can keep records. In this way, the students and teachers can find their preferable ones successfully.
